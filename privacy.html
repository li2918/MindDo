1. 我们收集的信息1. Information We Collect

当你注册账户、预约试课或填写评估时，我们会收集：When you sign up, book a trial, or complete an assessment, we collect:

  • 账户信息：学员姓名、邮箱、密码（加盐哈希存储）、家长联系方式。Account info: student name, email, password (stored as a salted hash), parent contact.
  • 课程记录：试课安排、选课结果、课堂反馈与学期报告。Course records: trial bookings, course selections, class feedback, and semester reports.
  • 使用数据：设备类型、浏览器、页面访问路径（需你同意 Cookie 才会收集）。Usage data: device type, browser, page visits (collected only after you accept cookies).

2. 信息如何使用2. How We Use It

  • 安排试课、匹配合适的老师，并向你发送课程确认邮件。Schedule trials, match a suitable instructor, and send confirmation emails.
  • 提供学员中心、课堂反馈、学期报告与客服支持。Operate the student hub, class feedback, semester reports, and customer support.
  • 改进课程质量与平台体验（仅使用去标识化的汇总数据）。Improve course quality and platform experience, using only aggregated, de-identified data.
  • 在你同意的前提下，向你发送新课程、优惠和活动通知。With your consent, send you updates on new classes, promotions, and events.

3. Cookie 与本地存储3. Cookies & Local Storage

我们使用必要 Cookie 来保持你的登录状态和语言偏好；分析类 Cookie 仅在你选择"全部接受"后才会启用。你可以在页面底部的 Cookie 横幅中随时调整。Essential cookies keep you signed in and remember your language. Analytics cookies activate only after you choose "Accept All". You can change this at any time via the banner at the bottom of any page.

4. 第三方服务4. Third-Party Services

我们仅使用为实现产品功能所必需的第三方服务（例如支付、邮件服务），并通过合同要求这些服务方达到同等的数据保护水平。We use third-party services only as needed to operate (e.g. payments, email delivery), and require contractual protections for equivalent data safeguards.

5. 儿童隐私 (COPPA)5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

MindDo 是面向 5–18 岁青少年的学习平台。对 13 岁以下的学员，我们在收集信息前将征得可验证的家长同意，仅收集与课程安排直接相关的信息，并限制对未成年人信息的使用范围。MindDo serves students aged 5–18. For students under 13, we obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting information, limit collection to what is strictly necessary for course delivery, and restrict how minors' data is used.

6. 你的权利6. Your Rights

  • 访问、更正或删除你的账户信息。Access, correct, or delete your account information.
  • 导出你的学习数据。Export your learning data.
  • 撤回同意并停止接收营销通讯。Withdraw consent and unsubscribe from marketing.
  • 向监管机构（如 FTC）投诉。File a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g. FTC).

7. 联系方式7. Contact

有任何隐私相关问题，请联系我们的数据保护负责人。For any privacy-related question, please contact our data protection officer.
