为什么需要它Why this exists

分数已不再是名校录取的唯一答案Test scores alone no longer move the needle.

招生官比从前更看重综合素质——研究能力、创新力、领导力与社会责任感。但真正成体系、有影响力的项目，需要清晰选题、专业指导与持续陪跑。 Admissions officers weigh research, creativity, leadership and social impact more than ever. Building a project deep enough to count on — that takes structure, mentoring and time.

现状Current state

学生独自做项目，痛点很现实Going it alone — the typical pitfalls

学业压力大、可投入时间被压缩；缺乏调研、立项、产出与发表的系统流程；活动堆叠没有故事主线，难以体现真实价值。 Heavy coursework leaves little time. Without a research-to-publication pipeline, activities pile up but never connect into a story admissions can buy.

我们的答案Our answer

「一个项目 × 多元成果」"One project, many outcomes."

兼具个性定制与系统化的背景提升项目：围绕一个核心项目，平行产出实习、论文、专利、竞赛、产品、品牌与社会应用等多维成果，让学生的成长轨迹清晰、可验证、可被招生官信服。 A single core project, fed through a structured pipeline that produces research, a paper, a patent, competition wins, a shipped product, a venture, and measurable social impact — a coherent admissions narrative, end to end.

项目框架Framework

从零 → 到一：12 步全程陪跑From zero to one — a 12-step guided path

每一步都明确产出与验收标准，把模糊的「背景提升」拆解成可执行、可量化的工程。 Every step has a defined deliverable and acceptance criteria — what is usually a vague "enrichment" effort becomes an engineered project.

01
🎯

个性定制Personalisation

了解学生未来发展方向、意向专业与兴趣爱好，画像清晰再启动。Map the student's goals, intended major and interests before kicking off.

02
🎓

匹配专业Major Fit

结合目标院校与意向专业的真实要求，规划项目方向与产出形态。Align the project shape with what target schools and majors actually look for.

03
🔍

开展调研Research

系统收集与分析资料，撰写可行性报告，为选题打下扎实依据。Survey the field, analyse prior work, ship a feasibility report.

04
📝

明确选题Topic Lock-in

基于调研结果定下研究方向，确保清晰、合理且具有发展潜力。Choose a topic that is novel, tractable and has room to grow.

05
🗺️

制定计划Roadmap

分阶段实施计划与里程碑，时间节点、任务拆解和成果要求一次说清。Stage-by-stage plan with milestones, owners and acceptance criteria.

06
🧪

参与实习Internship

匹配高效实验室进入真实科研环境，积累经验并获得正式实习证明。Match into a real lab, do real research, walk away with a verified credential.

07
📄

发表论文Publication

协助整理研究成果完成论文撰写，并推荐合适会议或期刊投稿。Help shape the manuscript and target the right venue or journal.

08
🏆

参加竞赛Competitions

筛选国内外权威赛事，从报名到答辩训练，把成果变成奖项。Pick the right tier-1 competitions; coach from entry through pitch.

09
🛠️

打造产品Product

将创意落地为原型，设计、开发、测试迭代，做到真实可用。Turn the idea into a working prototype — design, build, ship, iterate.

10
📑

申请专利Patent

提供专利检索、撰写与申请指导，让创新拿到法律与学术背书。Prior-art search, drafting and filing — turn the idea into a legal record.

11
🌱

创立品牌Brand / Venture

将产品孵化成创业项目：定位、商业模式、市场推广、运营全套。Spin the product into a real venture — positioning, model, GTM, ops.

12
🤝

社会应用 + 推荐Impact + Letters

在社区或真实场景中落地，申请总统义工奖；同步收获教授级推荐信。Deploy into a community, apply for the President's Volunteer Service Award, secure professor-level letters of recommendation.

常见问题FAQ

家长最常关心的几件事What parents ask first

这个项目适合哪些孩子？Who is this for?
从初中升高中、高中升本科到本科升硕博的所有阶段都覆盖；专业方向不限——理工科、人文社科、商科、艺术皆可。最重要的是孩子愿意围绕一个长期项目持续投入。 Anyone in a key transition window — middle-to-high, high-to-undergrad, undergrad-to-graduate. Open to STEM, humanities, business and the arts. The single requirement is that the student is willing to commit to one long-running project.
通常需要多长时间？How long does it take?
完整 12 步框架建议 9–18 个月，按学生现有进度可以并行加速。我们会先做一次免费的 30 分钟规划诊断，再给出适合的节奏。 A full 12-step run typically takes 9–18 months. Depending on what the student already has, parts can run in parallel. We start with a free 30-minute diagnosis to recommend the right pace.
没有编程或科研基础也可以参加吗？Can my child join without a coding / research background?
完全可以。MindDo 自有 100+ 门课程会按需补齐方法论，导师会从「调研 → 选题 → 立项」从头带。背景反而是次要因素，专注度和韧性更关键。 Absolutely. MindDo's 100+ in-house courses fill in any methodology gaps; mentors take the student from research → topic → project plan. Focus and grit matter more than prior credentials.
我能看到具体的费用吗？Can I see the price?
每位学生的项目方向、深度与成果组合都不同，我们不公开统一报价，而是根据规划诊断后给出对应方案。点击「联系顾问」即可与我们沟通。 Every student's project, depth and outcome mix differs, so we quote after the diagnostic call rather than publishing a fixed list. Tap "Talk to a counsellor" to start the conversation.
和市面上其他「背景提升」的差别？How is this different from other "enrichment" programs?
大多数项目是单点产品（只做竞赛、只做论文、只做实习）。MindDo 的核心是把它们串到一个项目里，让叙事自然连贯——这正是名校招生官想看到的「真实的人」。 Most programs are single-point products — competition only, paper only, internship only. MindDo's core idea is stringing them through one continuous project so the story holds together — exactly what admissions officers are reading for.

准备好和我们聊聊了吗？Ready to talk?

先来一次免费规划诊断 — 顾问会根据孩子目前的画像给出最适合的项目方向与节奏建议，不收取任何费用。 Start with a free planning call — your counsellor maps out a fitting project and tempo for the student. No charge, no pressure.
