合伙人常问FAQ

10 分钟扫完核心疑问The 10-minute partner FAQ

没有教育行业背景能加盟吗？Can I join without an education background?
可以。我们目前 12 家合伙校区中，有 7 家创始人来自互联网、零售、金融行业。教学品质由 MindDo 师资体系保障，合伙人重点负责本地运营、社区建设与团队管理。 Yes. 7 of our 12 partner sites have founders from tech, retail, or finance — not education. Teaching quality is held by MindDo's faculty system; partners drive local ops, community, and people management.
总投入 $200K 是必须的吗？Is the $200K total mandatory?
那是标准校区的参考预算。城市合伙人档总投入约 $40K 起；旗舰档 $400K+。我们会在签约前根据所在城市做更精确的测算。 That's the reference budget for a Standard center. Lean City Partner starts around $40K total; Flagship is $400K+. We tailor the number to your specific city before signing.
区域保护机制是怎样的？How does territory protection work?
合伙人档 1 mile 半径内不再开新店；标准档 3 mile；旗舰档 5 mile + 区域优先权。线上学员归属按家长居住地划分，归当地校区结算。 No new partner site within 1 mi for Partner tier, 3 mi for Standard, 5 mi for Flagship plus regional first-look. Online students are routed by parent residence and settled with the matching local center.
师资从哪里来？Where do teachers come from?
MindDo 提供两条路径：① 总部派驻首批 1–2 名核心讲师覆盖前 6 个月；② 合伙人本地招聘后，参加 5 周师训营 + 实习带教。两条路径可以叠加。 Two paths: (1) MindDo deploys 1–2 core instructors to seed the first 6 months; (2) you recruit locally, candidates go through our 5-week bootcamp + apprenticeship. Both paths can stack.
第 1 年招生大概能到多少？How many students should I expect in Year 1?
参考已开业的 12 家合伙校区，标准档第 1 年末平均 95 名在读学员、ramp 期收入 $340K 左右；旗舰档可到 150 名 / $530K。城市级别和合伙人本地资源会显著影响曲线。 Across our 12 active partner centers, Standard-tier sites average 95 active students by EOY1 and $340K Year-1 revenue; Flagship hits ~150 / $530K. City tier and local network materially shift the ramp.
合同期多长？退出机制？How long is the contract? Exit terms?
初始合同 5 年，期满可续签 5 年（不再收加盟费）。中途如需退出，已开业满 12 个月可申请转让；MindDo 享有优先回购权，按估值公式定价。 Initial term is 5 years, renewable for another 5 with no new franchise fee. Early exit is allowed after the 12-month operating mark; MindDo has right of first refusal at a formula-based valuation.
能跟其他教育品牌共享场地吗？Can I co-locate with another education brand?
看品类。和外语 / 艺术 / 体育类品牌共享场地是允许的（甚至鼓励，因为家长动线契合）；与其他 STEM / 编程 / AI 品牌共享需总部审批。 Depends on the category. Sharing space with language / art / sports schools is fine (and often encouraged — parent journeys overlap). Sharing with other STEM / coding / AI brands requires HQ approval.
